Consent Is Needed to Continue

To keep our AI tools transparent and safe, we have to store a small amount of information whenever you use Saathi:

  • We store chat inputs and generated documents to provide and improve the service.
  • We do not sell or share your information with third parties.
  • We assign a random anon_id to your device so your consent and rate limits work without needing your name or phone number.
  • You can request deletion at any time by emailing privacy@saathilegal.in.

If you decline consent, interactive features such as chat, document drafting, and calculators remain disabled.
