Saathi Legal Assistant

Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 7, 2025

1. Data We Collect

  • Profile details: Optional name and phone number you save for personalization.
  • Chat inputs: Prompts, follow-up questions, and generated responses.
  • Usage metadata: IP address, browser user agent, timestamps, and consent decisions.
  • Uploads & forms: Any text you provide inside calculators, templates, or case trackers.
  • Anonymous session IDs (anon_id): Random identifiers stored on your device so we can link consent, rate limits, and calculator usage without collecting personal information.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Deliver real-time answers, calculators, and document templates tailored to your input.
  • Improve model prompts, safety filters, and template accuracy (after removing personal identifiers).
  • Maintain audit logs for consent, abuse prevention, and service reliability.
  • Associate activity across sessions using anon_id instead of your phone or email so we can enforce rate limits and investigate abuse while keeping you pseudonymous.

3. Retention & Deletion

  • Chat transcripts and calculator inputs are retained for up to 180 days unless you request deletion sooner.
  • Consent logs are preserved for regulatory compliance for up to 2 years.
  • You can email privacy@saathilegal.in to delete or export your data.
  • Your anon_id lives inside your browser storage; clearing cookies/local storage or using a new device automatically resets it.

4. Security Measures

  • HTTPS is enforced end to end on Render hosting.
  • Secrets and API keys are stored in encrypted environment variables.
  • Access to logs and transcripts is restricted to vetted administrators following the principle of least privilege.

5. Sharing & Third Parties

  • AI processing is performed through Google Gemini APIs (Google may store prompts per their terms).
  • No marketing, resale, or advertising sharing occurs.
  • We will only disclose data if required by law or to investigate abuse.

6. Your Choices

  • Use Saathi in guest mode without saving your details.
  • Withdraw consent at any time through the consent modal or by contacting us.
  • Request data deletion or portability by emailing privacy@saathilegal.in.
  • Reset or rotate your anon_id anytime by clearing Saathi cookies/local storage or opening the tools in a private window.

Questions? Reach us at privacy@saathilegal.in.
