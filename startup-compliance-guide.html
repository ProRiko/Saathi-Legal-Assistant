Quarterly Compliance Checklist

Use Saathi templates and calculators to keep your company, investors, and employees aligned.

Founder & Cap Table

  • Execute founder service agreements and IP assignment (see Agreement Templates).
  • Document vesting schedules and cliff in ESOP letters.
  • Update cap table quarterly—store in Case Tracker attachments.

Labour & Payroll

  • Maintain attendance and wages registers per Shops & Establishments Act.
  • Use the Working Hours Calculator to flag overtime payouts.
  • Draft employment and contractor agreements via Saathi templates.

Data & Privacy

  • Publish privacy policy + ToS referencing DPDP Bill obligations.
  • Use Chat + Manual Review to craft incident notifications if breaches occur.
  • Track consent logs with Saathi's embedded consent modal.

ROC / FEMA Filings

  • Set reminders for AOC-4, MGT-7, PAS-3 depending on share allotments.
  • Store supporting board resolutions using template letters.
  • Use Case Tracker to assign owners for each compliance item.
