Consumer Protection

Fix Refunds & Service Failures

Use this 4-step workflow to escalate consumer complaints under the Consumer Protection Act 2019.

Estimate Compensation Send Demand Notice Find Legal Help

1. Collect Proof

  • Invoices, warranty cards, screenshots of chats, ticket numbers.
  • Photos/videos of the defect or service lapse.
  • Call logs showing escalation attempts.

2. Quantify Your Loss

Use Saathi’s Consumer Compensation Calculator to combine refund, delay, and incidental expenses. Export the breakdown into your notice.

Inputs needed: purchase amount, issue type, delay days, and out-of-pocket expenses.

3. Issue a Demand Notice

Generate the Consumer Complaint Legal Notice template and attach calculator outputs plus evidence list.

  • Address the seller/service provider with 15-day deadline.
  • Send via email + speed post, retain tracking receipts.

4. File With Consumer Commission (If Ignored)

Escalate on e-Daakhil or at your District Commission. Upload Saathi notice PDF, evidence bundle, and proof of delivery.

Jurisdiction:
  • Up to ₹50 lakh: District Commission
  • ₹50 lakh – ₹2 crore: State Commission
  • Above ₹2 crore: National Commission
